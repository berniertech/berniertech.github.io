Technical Support

Bernier Tech provides email-based technical support for all customers. We typically respond within 24–48 hours, with critical issues prioritised.

Support email: support@berniertech.com

Severity Levels

  • Sev-1: Critical impact – service unavailable or major outage. Response: Immediate priority.
  • Sev-2: Major functionality degraded. Response: Same day.
  • Sev-3: General queries or minor issues. Response: 24–48 hours.

What to Include in Your Request

  • Clear description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Relevant logs, screenshots, or error messages
  • Environment or configuration details
