Technical Support

Bernier Tech provides email-based technical support for all customers. Our goal is to ensure reliability, clarity, and timely assistance for every issue.

Support email: support@berniertech.com

Response Times

  • Sev-1: Critical impact – service unavailable or major outage. Response: **Immediate priority**.
  • Sev-2: Major functionality degraded. Response: **Same business day**.
  • Sev-3: Minor issues or general queries. Response **within 24–48 hours**.

Information to Include

To help resolve your issue quickly, please include:

  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Relevant logs, screenshots, or error messages
  • Environment or configuration details

Tiempos de Respuesta

Información Necesaria
