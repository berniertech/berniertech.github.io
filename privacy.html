Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Bernier Tech handles information when you interact with our website or contact us.

Information We Collect

  • Voluntary contact information such as email enquiries.
  • Basic website analytics such as page visits or browser type. No personal tracking or profiling is performed.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No customer production data.
  • No billing data.
  • No behavioural tracking, cookies, or personal identifiers beyond anonymous analytics.
  • No sharing or selling of data to third parties.

How We Use Information

  • Responding to enquiries.
  • Providing technical support.
  • Improving website content and performance.

Data Security

Bernier Tech uses modern security practices including encryption, restricted access, and secure cloud storage aligned with industry standards.

Customer Data in Cloud Services

For engineering or cloud engagements, customer-controlled environments remain under the customer’s responsibility unless otherwise agreed. Bernier Tech does not store or retain customer cloud data unless explicitly authorised and required for the service.

GDPR Compliance

Bernier Tech complies with the UK GDPR and EU GDPR frameworks. We only process personal information that you voluntarily provide, under the lawful basis of legitimate interest or consent depending on context.

Your GDPR Rights

  • The right to access your personal data.
  • The right to request correction or deletion.
  • The right to restrict or object to processing.
  • The right to data portability.

You may exercise these rights at: privacy@berniertech.com

Data Retention

We retain email correspondence only for as long as necessary to respond to enquiries or provide support. Information may be archived or deleted securely thereafter.
